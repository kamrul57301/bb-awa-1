Eco-Friendly Coatings, Engineered For Florida

AWA Coatings LLC is your trusted choice for advanced exterior coating systems in Orlando and Central Florida. Specializing in long-lasting exterior protection, the non-toxic coatings we use are designed to outperform traditional painting, delivering superior durability, energy efficiency, and curb appeal.

We prioritize environmentally responsible coating systems formulated with low VOC (Volatile Organic Compounds). These eco-conscious products help reduce environmental impact while promoting healthier indoor air quality for families, employees, and customers.

While we serve communities across the state, our team is dedicated to homeowners and businesses throughout Central Florida, with coating systems engineered specifically for our intense sun, humidity, and storms.
